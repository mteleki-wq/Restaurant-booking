Our Story

Begun with a single strand of pearls.

BitsNpieces started at a worn kitchen table, restringing a grandmother's pearls and falling for the patience the work asked for. We never lost that feeling — that the smallest, quietest things are the ones we keep.

Today we work with a handful of family ateliers across Europe and the South Pacific, choosing materials that age beautifully and refusing anything that doesn't. No seasons to chase. Just pieces made to outlast trends.
