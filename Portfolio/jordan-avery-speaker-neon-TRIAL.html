About the Speaker

Ideas that move people.

Jordan Avery is a dynamic keynote speaker, leadership strategist, and business educator known for delivering engaging presentations that inspire action and create lasting impact.

With extensive experience across education, business, leadership, and personal development, Jordan brings real-world insights combined with practical strategies that audiences can immediately apply, challenging conventional thinking and empowering meaningful growth.

Recognised for an authentic delivery style and the ability to simplify complex topics, every presentation is tailored to the audience — relevant, practical and inspiring, with value that lasts long after the event.
