About the Trainer

Helping you become stronger.

With over 10 years of experience in fitness coaching and personal development, Jordan Avery is passionate about helping individuals build strength, improve confidence, and create lasting lifestyle changes.

Specialising in personalised training programs, Jordan works with clients of all fitness levels — from beginners taking their first steps towards better health to experienced athletes striving for peak performance.

Whether your objective is weight loss, muscle development, improved mobility, athletic performance, or overall wellbeing, Jordan provides the support and accountability needed to achieve sustainable results.
