A teacher and trade-qualified chef whose culinary knowledge is built on a foundation of sound French techniques and traditions. With experience spanning bistro, four- and five-star hotels, Michelin-starred and hatted restaurants, James brings rare depth to both the kitchen and the classroom. Holding a Bachelor of Adult Education (Honours) from UTS, he currently serves as Trainer and Assessor at Le Culinaire Hospitality Institute — and runs his own chocolate and supply business, James Collins Trading.
