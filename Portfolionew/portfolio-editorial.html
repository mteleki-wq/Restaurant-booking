The story so far

Food as a feature story

Great food tells a story — of the season, the producer, and the person behind the pass. That belief has carried me from my first apprenticeship to leading brigades in some of Australia's most-loved dining rooms.

My career spans hatted restaurants, boutique hotels and large-scale events. Whether directing a team of twenty or composing an intimate tasting menu, the focus never changes: quality, precision, and a plate worth remembering.

Today I consult on menu development, lead kitchen teams through openings and transitions, and mentor the next generation of chefs.

The best dishes don't shout. They tell you quietly where they came from.
