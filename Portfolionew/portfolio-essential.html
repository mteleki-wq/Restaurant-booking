About

The short version

I believe great food tells a story — of the season, the producer, and the person behind the pass.

My career spans hatted restaurants, boutique hotels and large-scale events across Australia. Whether leading a brigade of twenty or designing an intimate tasting menu, my focus is the same: quality, precision and a plate you'll remember.

Today I consult on menu development, lead kitchen teams and mentor the next generation of chefs.
