About

A neighbourhood table with serious intent.

Maison Escapade began with a simple idea: cook what the season gives you, treat it with respect, and make everyone at the table feel looked after.

Our menus change with the markets. Our sittings are unhurried. The wine list leans local, the sourdough is baked at dawn, and the kitchen runs on curiosity as much as discipline.

Whether you're two at the bar or twelve for a birthday, the welcome is the same. Fifteen years on, that's still the whole recipe.
