About

The person behind the plates.

Great food tells a story — of the season, the producer, and the person behind the pass.

My career spans hatted restaurants, boutique hotels and large-scale events across Australia. Whether directing a brigade of twenty or composing an intimate tasting menu, the focus never changes: quality, precision and a plate worth remembering.

Today I consult on menu development, lead kitchen teams through openings and transitions, and mentor the next generation of chefs.
